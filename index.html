about me

I'm Sara, a Geospatial Analytics PhD student based in Raleigh, NC. My research interests include mitigating human morbidity and mortality related to environmental hazards (e.g., heat and flood) and pollution, as well as conservation and natural resources management.

My current research projects focus on data aggregation and tool design for industry professionals with little to no GIS experience.
